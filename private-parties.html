Treat Yourself to Sleepover Fun at Air Madness 

Anyone can have a sleepover at your home. How about telling your friends you are having a sleepover at the coolest place in town? Pack your blanket and pillow and spend the night with your best friends playing away until you drop at Air Madness. 

Get unlimited jumping to all the attractions in the park, including The Laser Maze, The Wipeout and The Bull Pen.

Lock-ins can be booked when the park is closed. The doors will be locked 30 minutes after the lock-in begins. We are priced to accommodate 50 to 400 people. Call us for more information. 

Sleepover Parties Include the Following

  • Air conditioning
  • Music
  • Cosmic lights that can be turned on at your request
  • Locked doors
  • The Food Court where you can purchase food and drinks throughout the night
  • Access to reserved party areas

Sleepover/Trampoline Parties Are Perfect For

  • High school senior night
  • Sweet 16 party
  • Team celebrations
  • Birthday celebration
  • Church groups
  • Youth groups
  • Youth associations
  • School groups
  • Sororities/fraternities
  • End-of-the-year party
  • After prom
  • Graduation
